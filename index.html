Outsourcing

At MIH, we harness our talents to help business achieve their corporate goals we are strong in converting business processes in an easy automated solution to achieve optimal productivity and efficiency.

Innovative Challanges

MIH challenge is for novel idea generation for budding entrepreneurs and innovators who come up with solutions that change the world and above all help the government and private sector in realizing the big four agenda. We intend to focus on health, manufacturing, agriculture and Art.

Enterpreneurship Training

Innovation in itself is incomplete without imparting the necessary skills to commercialize and monetize the inventions. At MIH, we partner with great industry players for knowledge transfer to enable the innovators refine and polish their business skills.

Code Kids

Exposing kids on the basics of coding is sure step in revolutionizing their thought process to be great thinkers. At MIH, early stage code training to young minds prepares the next thinkers for our ecosystem, we think about a conveyor belt of next startup champions.

Get to know us

We dream of changing the world through innovative and practical solutions that embrace human values and above all business ethics. At MIH, we incubate thoughts and don’t shy away to be crazy about ideas, but we also focus on bringing to reality the products and services of our ecosystem by simplifying the entrepreneurship skills and training thinkers.
